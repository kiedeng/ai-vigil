# Code of Conduct

AI Vigil follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Help maintain a friendly and productive project space.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participants who harm the project community.
